The Backend

What Happens When
a Lead Comes In

①
Captured
Lead submits contact info and investable assets inside the tool before results unlock. High-intent moment.
②
Logged
Data flows into a backend system managed by Lane6. Every lead is timestamped, tagged by tool source, and stored.
③
Qualified
Leads are filtered by investable asset threshold. Low-quality submissions don't waste advisor time.
④
Routed
Qualified leads are handed off to a Corelytics advisor for follow-up. Lane6 handles everything upstream.
Lane6 owns the infrastructure. Corelytics gets the qualified conversations. That's the deal.
